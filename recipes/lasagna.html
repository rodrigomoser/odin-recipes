Very Special Lasagna

Ingredients

Meat Sauce

  • 1 lb (450 g) ground beef
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 3 - 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 jar (24 oz / ~700 g) marinara sauce
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • Black pepper, to taste
  • Optional but great: a pinch of red pepper flakes

Cheese Layer

  • 15 oz (425 g) ricotta cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan
  • Salt & pepper

Layers

  • 9 - 12 lasagna noodles (regular or no-boil)
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella
  • Extra Parmesan for topping

Instructions

  1. Make the meat sauce

    Heat a large pan over medium heat. Brown the ground beef until cooked through. Add onion and cook 3–4 minutes until soft. Stir in garlic for 30 seconds. Add marinara, tomato paste, oregano, basil, salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes. Simmer 10 - 15 minutes, stirring occasionally.

  2. Mix the ricotta layer

    In a bowl, combine ricotta, egg, Parmesan, salt, and pepper. Set aside.

  3. Cook noodles (if needed)

    Boil lasagna noodles in salted water until just al dente. Drain and lay flat.

  4. Assemble the lasagna

    Preheat oven to 375°F / 190°C.

    In a 9×13 baking dish:

    • Spread a thin layer of sauce
    • Add noodles
    • Spread ricotta mixture
    • Add meat sauce
    • Sprinkle mozzarella

    Repeat layers until everything’s used, ending with sauce + mozzarella + Parmesan.

  5. Bake

    Cover with foil (tent it so cheese doesn’t stick).

    Bake 25 minutes covered, then 20 minutes uncovered until bubbly and golden.

  6. Rest (important!)

    Let it sit 10–15 minutes before slicing. This keeps it from collapsing into lasagna soup.

Optional upgrades

  • Add sautéed mushrooms or spinach to the sauce
  • Swap beef for Italian sausage (or half & half)
  • Use béchamel instead of ricotta for a more Italian-style vibe
